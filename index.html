Let me tell you a little bit more about myself

I’ve always had a creative side to me. As a kid I loved to draw, do arts and crafts, make up outrageous imagionary stories... over time I started to think of better ways to put this creativity to use.

I started to research web design (what the heck is this HTML and CSS nonesense they speak of?) and the more I learned, the more I became interested. I started to look into design, playing with colours and fonts, trying to find ways to use this new skill. When it came time to go to College and my dad suggested the Graphic Design program, it was a no brainer. Here I am today, this passion for creativity only grows more and more. Allow me to share my imagination and creativity with you.
