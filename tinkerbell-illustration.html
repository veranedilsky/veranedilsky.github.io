Hello, my name is Vera and I am creative

I’ve always had a creative side to me. As a kid I loved to draw, do arts and crafts, make up outrageous imagionary stories... over time I started to think of better ways to put this creativity to use. I started to research web design (what the heck is this HTML and CSS nonesense they speak of?) and the more I learned, the more I became interested. I started to look into design, playing with colours and fonts, trying to find ways to use this new skill. When it came time to go to College and my dad suggested the Graphic Design program, it was a no brainer. Here I am today, this passion for creativity only grows more and more. Allow me to share my imagination and creativity with you.

AF-Clean Brochure

One of the many projects I had at Walter Surface Technologies, this required me to focus more on their Bio-Circle line which is for industrial cleaners. We had to create a brochure for a new product they would be selling.

"Veroll" Bowling Brochure

This was only a small part to a big project. We were required to chose either a fictional or real company and rebrand it, creating many types of promotional material. For this project, we chose to go with a 50's theme, featuring pin-up girls and pastel colors. This was a brochure we create to promote the bowling alley.
