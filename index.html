Hello, my name is Vera and I am creative

I’ve always had a creative side to me. I started to look into design, playing with colours and fonts, trying to find ways to use this new skill. When it came time to go to College and my dad suggested the Graphic Design program, it was a no brainer. Here I am today, this passion for creativity only grows more and more. Allow me to share my imagination and creativity with you.
